Focus of the senior refresh

The Stage 6 roadmap consolidates feedback from staff workshops and advisory partners. Each focus area below will eventually open into a dedicated Construction-style module with downloadable artefacts, contextual resources, and exemplar folios.

Priority areas

Status tags match the Construction roadmap so progress can be compared at a glance.

Focus Area Key Actions Status
Industry Context Refresh Update contextual studies to feature renewable energy, transport infrastructure, and automation. Link each context to industry interviews and case studies similar to the Construction hub stories. Drafting
Evidence Collection Toolkit Adapt Construction observation checklists, folio exemplars, and assessment rubrics to Engineering criteria. Bundle downloads to suit each major project milestone. Scoping
Workshop Modernisation Map required tooling upgrades and maintenance schedules. Photo essays and VR walkthroughs will introduce the revised lab layouts before practical lessons commence. In progress
Capstone Project Pathways Define optional capstone themes (sustainable homes, robotics, mobility devices) and align them with folio checkpoints, theory modules, and community partnerships. Queued
