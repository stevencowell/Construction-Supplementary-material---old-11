Delivery pillars

Focus areas that keep Engineering in lockstep with Construction.

Digital Content Production

  • Film workshop induction clips and edit into short safety reels.
  • Storyboard microlearning videos for CAD, coding, and testing procedures.
  • Embed audio descriptions to support inclusive delivery.

Curriculum Alignment

  • Map every Engineering outcome to Construction equivalents for cross-disciplinary projects.
  • Coordinate assessment windows to reduce clashes across VET courses.
  • Publish a combined evidence tracker for student folios.

Platform Enhancements

  • Deploy shared search index spanning Construction and Engineering modules.
  • Enable analytics to surface the most accessed resources.
  • Introduce user role controls for student-only versus teacher-only content.
