Learning Intent

Give students hands-on experience with automation components while embedding systems thinking. Lessons emphasise diagnosing faults, calibrating sensors, and iterating coded solutions.

Core Objectives

  • Interpret circuit schematics and breadboard equivalent layouts.
  • Write and document simple control algorithms using microcontrollers.
  • Evaluate system performance against specified user requirements.

Planned Evidence

  • Annotated wiring diagrams and flowcharts.
  • Demonstration videos highlighting iterative debugging.
  • Student reflection linking automation solutions to industry contexts.
