Learning Intent

Establish a culture of safety and design thinking within the workshop. Students complete micro-challenges that surface measurement, materials, and evaluation skills required across later tasks.

Core Objectives

  • Demonstrate safe equipment setup using WHS routines aligned with Construction expectations.
  • Apply precise measurement conversions between metric and imperial units.
  • Document design iterations with quick sketching and annotation techniques.

Planned Evidence

  • Safety passport checklist signed off by teacher.
  • Photographic log of iterative prototypes.
  • Reflection prompts aligning habits of mind to curriculum outcomes.
