Learning Intent

Establish a culture of safety and design thinking within the workshop. Students complete micro-challenges that surface measurement, materials, and evaluation skills required across later tasks.

Core Objectives

  • Demonstrate safe equipment setup using WHS routines aligned with Construction expectations.
  • Apply precise measurement conversions between metric and imperial units.
  • Document design iterations with quick sketching and annotation techniques.

Planned Evidence

  • Safety passport checklist signed off by teacher.
  • Photographic log of iterative prototypes.
  • Reflection prompts aligning habits of mind to curriculum outcomes.

Sequence at a Glance

Week-by-week overview of how the Foundations task scaffolds skills prior to the Structures pathway.

Week 1 · Safety & Space Setup

Live
  • White card refresher and lab zoning walk-through.
  • Issue safety passport checklist via OneNote.
  • Mini challenge: tool ID relay with photo evidence.

Week 2 · Measurement Mastery

Drafting
  • Interactive conversions carousel (metric & imperial).
  • Introduce callipers and verniers with rotation stations.
  • Prototype logbook entry demonstrating tolerance checks.

Week 3 · Rapid Prototyping Sprint

Queued
  • Design-fix-reflect loop across three micro challenges.
  • Introduce reflection prompts for engineering habits of mind.
  • Compile evidence bundle ready for Structures handover.
