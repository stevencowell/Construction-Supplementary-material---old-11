Learning Intent

Students investigate how engineering systems transfer forces through beams, trusses, gears, and linkages. Inquiry tasks encourage experimentation with load, stability, and efficiency.

Core Objectives

  • Analyse how shape and material choice influence structural performance.
  • Prototype mechanisms that convert motion or alter speed and torque.
  • Use engineering journals to justify design iterations with data.

Planned Evidence

  • Testing data from beam deflection and bridge load trials.
  • Annotated folio pages summarising mechanism prototypes.
  • Peer critique notes aligned with industry communication skills.
